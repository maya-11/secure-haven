FEATURES

Why Choose Us

Experienced Staff

Our experienced staff provides empathetic support, crisis intervention, legal guidance, safety planning, and cybersecurity measures to protect women from stalkers. Our multilingual team ensures inclusivity, while public awareness initiatives educate the community. We strive to create a safe haven for women, offering comprehensive support and protection through our website.

Emergency Assistance

In urgent situations, our emergency assistance team is available 24/7 to provide immediate support and help women navigate through critical stalking incidents. Your safety is our top priority, and we are just a call away when you need us the most.

Online Booking for Consultation

We offer convenient online booking for one-on-one consultations with our experts. Whether you need legal/illegal advice, safety planning, or to get rid of someone, you can schedule consultation session at your preferred time from the comfort of your home.

OUR SERVICES

How We Can Help

Personalized Protection

We provide personalized safety planning and protection strategies to ensure your safety and well-being. Our experts will work with you to create a plan tailored to your specific needs.

Emotional Support

Our caring and empathetic team offers emotional support and counseling to help you cope with the impact of stalking. We are here to listen and provide a safe space for you to express your feelings.

Legal Guidance

If you need legal assistance, we can guide you through the process of obtaining restraining orders and pressing charges against your stalker. We'll help you understand your legal rights and options.
